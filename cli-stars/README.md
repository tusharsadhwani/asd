# cli-stars

Print stars on your CLI.

## Usage

```sh
> python stars.py 4
         *
        * *
       * * *
* * * * * * * * * *
 * * * * * * * * *
  * * * * * * * *
   * * * * * * *
  * * * * * * * *
 * * * * * * * * *
* * * * * * * * * *
       * * *
        * *
         *
```

```sh
> python stars.py --david 6
               *
              * *
             *   *
            *     *
           *       *
* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
 *       *           *       *
  *     *             *     *
   *   *               *   *
    * *                 * *
     *                   *
    * *                 * *
   *   *               *   *
  *     *             *     *
 *       *           *       *
* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *
           *       *
            *     *
             *   *
              * *
               *
```

```sh
> python stars.py --help
```
